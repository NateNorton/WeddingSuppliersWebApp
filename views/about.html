Home Connect. Your home, at your fingertips

About us

Home Connect is an IOT devices management online tool developed by Susanna Ventafridda, a second year Computer Science student at Goldsmiths College, University of London.

The application allows to safely interact and monitor home devices such as heating systems, light and media appliances through a unified and well-integrated platform.
